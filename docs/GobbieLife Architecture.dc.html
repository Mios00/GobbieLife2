RUNS ALL THREE ERAS ↓
◇ HIDDEN ALIGNMENT
Four axes — Aggr · Dipl · Isol · Hero — accrue silently from choices. Never shown as bars. Decides the ending.
◈ THE ORACLE
Riddles that hint at the future you're drifting toward — the only feedback on hidden Alignment.
≈ WHISPERS
Ambient flavor — combinatorial + authored + cast/faction + rare Hollow lines. All era-scaled, all tone-aware.
◆ RECKONINGS
Periodic choice-events (~130 across the run). Move resources and quietly tip Alignment. Era-scaled to what goblins care about; ~1 in 6 is a gamble (good/bad coin-flip), and Calamities escalate era to era.
▤ THE CHRONICLE
The saga log — colour-coded by kind, exportable & shareable from the first minute.
⚖ THE STANDINGS · from Era II
Four powers — Sunspire · Iron States · Dwarrow Holds · Splinter Packs. Reckonings, raids & dedicated attitude events (warm/cold choices, ~9 scenarios per power) shift them; hostility brings war.
◍ THE HOLLOW · from 2.3
Mythic lore behind the mist — grows from rare whispers into Era III's Dark Beyond doom clock.
⚑ THE WANDER
Parallel 9-quest chain, 4 variants each, with road choices, riddles & minigames.
